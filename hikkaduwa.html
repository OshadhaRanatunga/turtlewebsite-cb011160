HIKKADUWA TURTLE HATCHERY, SRI LANKA

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is a conservation project located on the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka. It was established in 1986 with the aim of protecting sea turtles from extinction and conserving their natural habitats.
